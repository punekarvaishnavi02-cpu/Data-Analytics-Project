Product Inventory Analysis.¶

Introduction¶

This project analyzes a product inventory dataset with details like category, price, stock quantity, manufacturing/expiration dates, and customer ratings. The goal is to clean, explore, and visualize the data to identify trends, detect low-stock or expired items, and evaluate product performance. Insights from this analysis can help optimize inventory management and decision-making.
